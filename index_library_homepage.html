Contact Tracing and Quarantine Strategy Case Study Library

A source-traceable case study library documenting real-world contact tracing and quarantine strategies across countries and infectious disease outbreaks.

About this library

This library compiles real-world policy examples in a structured and comparable format, including system design, implementation phases, quarantine rules, key quantitative evidence, limitations, and potential implications for infectious disease modelling.

The aim is to support policy comparison, evidence synthesis, and the development of model parameters for outbreak control strategies.

Work in progress: This case study library is currently under development. If you notice incorrect information, missing context, or broken links, please contact the compiler or open an issue on GitHub.
